Welcome to Brain Training Games!

📖 About This App

Brain Training Games is designed to help you exercise your mind through fun and engaging puzzles. Keep your brain sharp while having fun!

🎮 Available Games

🎴 Memory Matching

Test your memory by finding matching pairs of cards. Flip two cards at a time and remember their positions to match all pairs in the fewest moves possible!

📝 Word Search Puzzle

Find hidden words in a grid of letters. Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Choose from themed word lists or create your own custom puzzles with photos!

✨ Key Features

  • Multiple Game Modes: Play with emojis, themed words, or upload your own photos
  • Customizable Difficulty: Choose between easy and hard modes to match your skill level
  • Photo Mode: Upload personal images with custom labels for a personalized experience
  • Save & Load: Save your custom photo sets and load them anytime you want to play
  • Multiple Themes: Word search includes Animals, Fruits, Family, and Nature themes
  • Touch & Mouse Support: Play seamlessly on any device - desktop, tablet, or phone

🎯 How to Play

  1. Click "Let's Start Playing!" to access the main game menu
  2. Select a game from the available options (Memory Matching or Word Search)
  3. Choose your preferred mode (emojis/photos or themes) and difficulty level
  4. For Photo Mode: Upload images and add labels - you can save these sets for future games
  5. Click "Start Game" and enjoy! Track your progress as you play
  6. Complete the puzzle to see your congratulations message and final score

💡 Pro Tips

  • In Memory Matching, try to remember card positions even when they don't match
  • In Word Search, words can appear forwards or backwards - scan carefully!
  • Look for unusual letter combinations to spot hidden words more easily
  • Photo Mode works great for learning - use family photos, vocabulary words, or study material
  • Save your favorite photo sets to play again without re-uploading
  • Start with Easy mode to learn the game, then challenge yourself with Hard mode
